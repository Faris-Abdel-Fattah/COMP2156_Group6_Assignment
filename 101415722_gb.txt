# George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
# It was founded in 1967 and is named after George Brown, a prominent 19th-century Canadian politician and newspaper publisher.
# The college offers a wide range of programs including certificates, diplomas, degrees, and postgraduate certificates in fields such as business, community services, culinary arts, design, and health sciences.
# George Brown may not seem as new as other schools, but it's really great! There's a ton to learn here!
